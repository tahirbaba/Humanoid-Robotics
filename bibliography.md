## Citation Standards

All citations in this course follow APA 7th edition format:

**Journal Article:**
Author, A. A., Author, B. B., & Author, C. C. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Periodical, volume*(issue), pages. https://doi.org/xxx

**Conference Paper:**
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of paper. In *Title of Conference Proceedings* (pp. page range). Publisher. https://doi.org/xxx

**Book:**
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of book*. Publisher. https://doi.org/xxx

**Online Documentation:**
Organization. (Year). *Title of documentation*. Retrieved from URL

---

## Source Quality Standards

Per the course constitution, all sources meet the following criteria:
- Peer-reviewed journal articles from IEEE, ACM, Springer, Nature, or Science
- Conference proceedings from top-tier venues (NeurIPS, ICRA, CoRL, RSS, IROS)
- Official technical documentation from authoritative organizations
- Government/military technical reports (DARPA, DoD, NIST)
- Manufacturer datasheets and technical specifications

Sources explicitly excluded:
- Blog posts (unless from recognized experts with peer-reviewed work)
- Wikipedia (may inform research but requires primary source verification)
- Marketing materials without independent validation
- Social media posts
- Unattributed web content
